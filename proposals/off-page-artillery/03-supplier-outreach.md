# 03 · Supplier & Manufacturer Outreach

**Goal**: get Jentech listed as an authorised installer / service agent on supplier websites. These pages typically rank well for *"[brand] installer Brisbane"*, *"[brand] authorised service agent"* type queries, and the link back to Jentech carries strong vertical authority.

The strongest of these is **PSS Distributors** — Jentech is already an authorised UPS service agent, so the listing should be a request, not a pitch.

---

## Priority targets

| # | Supplier | Why | Page type to request |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **PSS Distributors** | Authorised UPS service agent | "Find a service agent" / dealer locator |
| 2 | **MM Electrical** | Major electrical wholesaler Jentech buys from | Trade-account customer listings if any |
| 3 | **Daikin Australia** | Air conditioning installs (residential & commercial) | Authorised installer locator |
| 4 | **Mitsubishi Heavy Industries (MHI)** | A/C installs | Same |
| 5 | **Fujitsu Australia** | A/C installs | Same |
| 6 | **Clipsal / Schneider Electric** | Switchboards, GPOs, lighting | Trade partner / authorised installer lists |
| 7 | **Brilliant Lighting** | Lighting fittings (frequent on residential jobs) | Trade partner if available |
| 8 | **Energex** | Connections / meter relocations | "Approved electrical contractor" listing if available |

---

## Reality check

Most supplier directories are actively maintained but slow-moving. Expect:
- PSS Distributors → likely existing listing or quick add (Jentech is genuinely an authorised agent)
- Daikin / MHI / Fujitsu → installer locators are real and high-traffic; takes a form submission and possibly a sales-team contact
- MM Electrical / Clipsal / Brilliant → less likely to have a public installer directory, but worth asking

3–5 successful listings out of 8 attempts is a solid outcome.

## To-do before sending

For each supplier:
- Find the right page on their site (search `"[supplier] installer locator"` or `"[supplier] authorised dealer"`)
- Note the contact form / email / phone
- For Daikin / MHI / Fujitsu specifically, the regional sales rep is usually the unlock — Andrew may already have one
