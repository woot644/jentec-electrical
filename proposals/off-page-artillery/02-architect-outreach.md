# 02 · Architect Outreach

**Goal**: 6–10 high-quality vertical backlinks from Brisbane architects and designers Jentech has actually worked with. These are the highest-value links available — same-vertical, named-collaborator, project-substantiated — exactly what Google rewards in local pack ranking.

**Why now**: every architect listed below appears on a Jentech project page. The new live case studies are the hook for the outreach. *"We've published the project we did together — would you link to it?"* is a natural ask, not cold.

## To-do before sending

For each target, fill in:
- Contact name (principal / studio manager)
- Email address (often the studio's general inbox; better if there's a named contact from the original job)
- Studio website URL — confirm they have a "press" or "projects" section we can link from

This is a 30-minute pull from the existing project files, the architects' websites, and Jentech's email history with each. Andrew or Rachel will know the right contact in most cases.

## Tracking

Per architect: date sent, response (yes / no / no reply), link delivered (URL), date link went live. Architects who agree but haven't actioned by week 2 → polite nudge. No reply by week 3 → second touch.

A 30% response rate on this kind of warm outreach is realistic. 6–10 attempts → 2–4 actual backlinks. From this vertical, that's outsized.
